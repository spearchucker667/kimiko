# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming, professional environment for everyone.

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Focus on technical merit and collaborative problem-solving.
- No harassment, discrimination, doxxing, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting Issues

Report security issues privately via [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).  
For other conduct concerns, open a GitHub issue or contact the maintainers directly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including temporary or permanent ban from the project, in response to violations.
